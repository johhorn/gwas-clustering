Code for clustering of diseases and traits

This page describes the GitHub repository for the master thesis "Methods for representation and comparison of diseases and traits", written by Johanne H. Horn at the research group of Biomedical Informatics (BMI), University of Oslo (UiO).

The majority of the implementation is part of the Genomic HyperBrowser, an open source, web-based software system for comparative genomic analysis. The HyperBrowser is developed and supported by the BMI research group at UiO, and the Radium Hospital in Oslo. The tools and statistics provide different implementations for feature representation and comparison of genomic tracks. The tracks represent diseases and traits, and represented by associated variants from Genome-Wide Association Studies (GWAS). In addition to HyperBrowser code, scripts for LD expansion of variants, as given by their rsids, is provided.
